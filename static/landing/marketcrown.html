“Forecast, Improve, Monetize, Globally”

Forecast stock movement in multiple markets (NYSE, Nasdaq, Toronto Stock Exchange and HKSE)

Here is How it Works:
Users have the ability to forecast any of the 4 four global stock markets.
Forecasting involves a user stating a stocks position at a future date relative to where it is now.
Creating a forecast consists of entering a stock symbol(i.e. $AMZN), selecting direction (up/down), percentage movement (.2-.9%, 1-5%, 6-15%, 16-30%, 31+%), time of day(morning/midday/close) and date.
For example, On 01/12/16 $STOCK was at $56/share
On 01/12/16 Mike forecasts that $STOCK will be up 1-5% from its current price of $56/share during Midday on 01/17/16
The platform validates the forecast during the midday time frame on 01/17/16 to determine if the forecast is true.
